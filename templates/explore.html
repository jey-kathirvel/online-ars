Tea-covered mountains, wildlife-rich forests, sacred temples and refreshing waterfalls—all within reach of Akshat Royal Stay.

Recommended day trips

Wake up here.
Explore everywhere.

Our location in Bodinayakanur makes a beautiful base for discovering Tamil Nadu and Kerala. Start early, spend the day in the hills, and return to a comfortable room by evening.

We can help guests plan sightseeing routes and arrange dependable local travel.

01

Approximately 52 km · Full-day tour

Munnar

One of South India’s most loved hill stations, Munnar unfolds through endless tea plantations, mist-covered mountains, winding roads and waterfalls. Enjoy the cool climate, famous viewpoints and local cuisine before returning comfortably by evening.

Tea country · Mountain views · Waterfalls

02

Around 2 hours · Hill-station escape

Kodaikanal

Take a peaceful walk along Coaker’s Walk, enjoy boating on Kodaikanal Lake and admire panoramic views from Pillar Rocks. Pine forests, waterfalls, flower gardens and lively local markets make every visit memorable.

Lake · Pine forests · Viewpoints

03

Kerala highlands · Nature and wildlife

Idukki

Discover the iconic Idukki Dam, Ramakkalmedu’s sweeping views, spice plantations and the wildlife-rich forests around Periyar. Serene landscapes and celebrated eco-tourism experiences make Idukki ideal for a rewarding day excursion.

Dams · Spice country · Eco-tourism

04

High Wavy Mountains · Hidden paradise

Meghamalai

Climb scenic ghat roads into rolling valleys, tranquil reservoirs and cool mountain air. Part of the ecosystem surrounding the Srivilliputhur Meghamalai Tiger Reserve, this quiet landscape rewards nature lovers and photographers at every turn.

Tea estates · Wildlife · Panoramas

05

Theni district · Sacred heritage

Temple trail

Visit Veerapandi Gowmariyamman Temple on the banks of the Mullai River, then seek blessings at Kuchanur Saneeswaran Temple—one of India’s rare shrines where Lord Saneeswaran is worshipped as the presiding deity.

Veerapandi · Kuchanur · Living traditions

Wildlife · Boating · Spice gardens

Gardens · Family outing · Views

Sunrise · Mountain roads · Tea
